The Story Behind AmplifyU
Jenni Singh
Founder, AmplifyU Technologies Ltd · Digital Transformation Leader

I spent years working in digital transformation and leadership environments, helping organisations navigate change, build teams, and develop people.

One pattern kept repeating.

The professionals who advanced fastest weren't always the most technically capable. They were the ones who could communicate with clarity, influence decisions, and make their value visible.

Then I returned from maternity leave.

With two young boys at home and a career that mattered deeply to me, I could no longer rely on working longer hours. Every meeting, conversation, and opportunity had to count.

That's when I realised something powerful:

Communication is the skill that multiplies everything else.

When you communicate well, you work faster, influence more, build stronger relationships, and create opportunities. You don't just perform better at work — you come home with something left to give.

I built AmplifyU because the tools I needed didn't exist. Not another course. Not another workshop.

A practical, AI-powered coaching platform designed to help busy professionals master the skills that shape careers: storytelling, influence, executive presence, visibility, confidence, and leadership communication.

Because career-defining skills shouldn't be reserved for those with the right coach, the right manager, or the right network. They should be available to everyone.

AmplifyU was built in the margins of a full life. For people building one.
