Founder, AmplifyU Technologies Ltd · Digital Transformation Leader

I spent years working in digital transformation and leadership environments — partnering with organisations navigating complex change, building teams, and developing people.

One pattern kept repeating: the professionals who advanced weren't always the most technically capable. They were the ones who could communicate with clarity, command a room, and make their value visible.

Returning from maternity leave — and navigating career progression alongside family life — made one thing clear: traditional coaching is inaccessible to most. Too expensive. Too inconsistent. Too dependent on who you happen to know.

I built AmplifyU to change that. To make career-defining communication skills available to every professional — not just those with the right budget or the right manager.
