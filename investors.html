The Founder

Jenni Singh

Founder, CEO & Director · AmplifyU Technologies Ltd

I spent years in digital transformation and leadership environments, helping organisations navigate change, build teams, and develop people. One pattern kept repeating: the professionals who advanced fastest weren't always the most technically capable. They were the ones who could communicate with clarity, influence decisions, and make their value visible.

When I returned from maternity leave with two young children and a career that mattered deeply to me, I realised that communication wasn't just another skill — it was the multiplier behind every other skill I had. I built AmplifyU because the tools I needed didn't exist.

For Training Providers

Already working in
facilitated group settings

AmplifyU isn't just for individual learners. Training providers and L&D coaches can run it as a structured cohort programme — leading participants through the science and theory together, with each person completing the AI-coached practice privately on their own device.

01

Trainer leads the group

A coach or facilitator walks participants through each module using AmplifyU on screen — the science, the theory, the examples. Structured content, no separate preparation required.

02

Participants practice privately

Each person completes the AI-coached exercises on their own device. The practice is personal, not performed — no group exposure required, which is precisely what makes it effective.

03

No integration required today

Each participant uses their own account independently. The format works immediately — no IT procurement, no system integration, no lengthy procurement cycle to get started.

On the roadmap: Cohort reporting, manager dashboards, and enterprise SSO are planned for 2027 — built for how L&D teams will want to buy at scale.
